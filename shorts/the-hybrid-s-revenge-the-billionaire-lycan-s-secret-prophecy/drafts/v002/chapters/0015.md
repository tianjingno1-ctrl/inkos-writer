# 第15章 New Dawn

Six months passed.
The new council was running smoothly. Hybrids served as judges and generals. The old laws were dismantled piece by piece.
Ella’s belly swelled. She was pregnant—a child from the bonding. The prophecy had hinted at it, but she hadn’t believed until the healers confirmed.
Elena stayed in the palace, regaining strength to guide her daughter through the reigns.
One evening, Ella stood on the balcony, Ethan beside her.
“Are you afraid?” he asked.
“Of childbirth? Yes. Of the world we’re building? No.”
“The extremists are still out there. They tried another attack last week.”
“We stopped them. We’ll keep stopping them.”
Ethan nodded.
She looked at the moon. It was full, silver light spilling over the city.
“I used to dream of a life like this,” she said. “A life where I didn’t have to hide. Now I have it.”
“You earned it.”
“We earned it.”
She felt movement in her belly. A flutter.
“Our child is strong.”
“He or she will be the first of a new generation. True equal.”
Ella smiled. “The prophecy ends with us. No more blood feuds. No more curses.”
They stood in silence, watching the moon.
Then a knock came. Jason appeared. “Queen Ella, the emissary from the eastern coast has arrived. They want to join the charter.”
Ella sighed, pleased. “Let them in. Tomorrow we will conduct the ceremony.”
She turned to go inside, but paused for a final look at the sky.
*Thank you, Mother. Thank you for the prophecy.*
*Thank you for never giving up.*
She walked inside, hand in hand with Ethan, toward a kingdom at peace.
And in her heart, she knew: the hybrid’s revenge had become something greater—a legacy of unity and hope.
THE END
